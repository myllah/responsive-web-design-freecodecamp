Patrick Jane

Patrick Jane is a fictional character and the protagonist of the CBS crime drama The Mentalist, portrayed by Simon Baker.

After losing his wife and daughter, killed by the serial killer Red John, Patrick Jane seeks to avenge his family by killing Red John. In this way, he founds himself with motivation to join the CBI (California Bureau of Investigation), so that he can use his skills to help.

Jane is an independent consultant for the California Bureau of Investigation (CBI). He uses his tricks, observations, knowledge of psycology and mind games to give advices and insights on cases.

He feels guilty for the murder of his family. Patrick regained self-respect when he realized that his mentalist skills, that has resulted in the death of his family, could be used for good by aprehending bandits.

  • Birth: The date of birth of Patrick Jane is not clear. In the episode Byzantium (S07E11), the birth date on his driver's license is given as July 30, 1969. In the series finale, White Orchids (S07E13), the birth date given on his marriage license is September 16, 1974.
  • Infance: Alex Jane, Patrick Jane's father, exploited his abilities to make money by deceiving people. Patrick had what his father called "x-ray eyes," his incredible skills of observation, deduction and induction.
  • Marrige: Patrick meets his future wife, Angela Ruskin and has a daughter named Charlotte. After losing his family, he keeps the ring until he finally takes the ring off and gives it to Teresa Lisbon.
  • Life as a psychic: Patrick Jane was a scam, pretending to be a psychic. He used his manipulation skills to make families trust every word he said. On one of the talkshows he guest stared on, Patrick insulted a serial killer known as Red John. In revenge, Red John brutally killed his wife and daugther, leaving his signature, a smiley face on the wall.
  • Mental breakdown: After losing his family, Patrick Jane had a mental breakdown, he spent six months in a psychiatric ward where he was treated by Sophie Miller, who helped him to get better.
  • CBI: A year after his family were killed, Jane went to the California Bureau of Investigation (CBI) to get information about the Red John case. Determined to get information, he goes to a crime scene with Lisbon, so that she can answer his questions. Using his skills, ended up helping to solve the case and was hired as a consultant.
  • Characteristic: Jane's most known characteristic is the fact that he loves tea, appearing almost always with his cup in his hand. He is a bit eccentric, very intelligent, perceptive and insightful, however sometimes he is closed, arrogant and childish.
